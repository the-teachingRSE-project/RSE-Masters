\begin{frame}{First Issues and Current Answers}
\phantomsection\label{first-issues-and-current-answers}
\begin{enumerate}
\tightlist
\item
  Is RSE a CS study program? Yes, but not only
\item
  What is the BA requirement (audience)? CS or MNT depending on profile
\item
  Is RSE a recomposition of existing lectures? yes, but only partially
\item
  What is the intended job profile (if any)? Academia and research
  institutes primarily
\item
  Is the Curriculum one idea or a set of ideas? At least two ideas are
  dominating
\end{enumerate}
